EACH DAY (45–60 min session):

  1. Open the Daily Log tab. Today's row is highlighted.
  2. Tap Done, Partial, or Skip when you finish.
  3. Log any mistakes in the Error Log tab (60 seconds, single line).

EVERY WEEK (Sunday, ~15 min):

  1. Take the scheduled quiz from the Weekly Quizzes tab. Enter the score as a decimal (0.80 = 80%).
  2. Update Chapter Mastery for chapters covered this week.
  3. Flip through the Error Log. Mistakes seen 3+ times → next week's theme.

END OF SUMMER (mid-July):

  1. Take the comprehensive mock final (Week 8 of Phase 2 — already scheduled in the Daily Log).
  2. If score ≥ 85% AND solid on Ch 7-8 fundamentals → ready for 7th-grade Geometry.
  3. If 70-84% → ready but fragile, continue tutoring through the year.
  4. If < 70% → revisit the repeat conversation.

Color codes:

  • Highlighted blue border = today's session
  • Yellow rows = rest days (school's out, take the break)
  • Faded rows = past days you didn't mark

Data & reset:

All progress is saved automatically on this device only (in your browser's local storage). If you clear browser data, the tracker resets.
